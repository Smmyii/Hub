How the hook wiring works

# ~/.claude/settings.json — added automatically at setup
"Stop": [{ "command": "wm-notify waiting" }]
"SessionStart": [{ "command": "wm-notify active" }]
# wm-notify talks to the plugin via: zellij pipe --plugin workspace-manager
